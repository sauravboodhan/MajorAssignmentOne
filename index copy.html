Surrealism Origins

The Surrealist art movement began in the late 1910s, originating during a War-torn world. The artists of this moevement sought to liberate their minds & human experiance from the oppression of rationalism & reality. With this, the art pieces produced by some notable artists of this time have explored the subconcious, flamboyant, mysterious, and often, if not, fantastical elements of one's creativity.
